Department of Applied Mechanics, VNIT Nagpur

Foreign Delegates

Students and Research Scholars USD 150

  • This registration fee includes 18% GST charges.
  • Only one paper presentation is allowed under one student registration.
  • At least one of the authors of every paper must register for the conference as an author and present the paper in order for it to be included in the conference proceedings of NCWE-25.
  • If more than one author of a paper wishes to attend the conference, all such authors need to be registered separately by paying applicable registration fees.
  • Certificate will be provided only to the presenting author.
  • Please note that no refund will be made in case of cancellation of registrations.

Payment Gateway:

  1. Go to https://pay.vnit.ac.in/home
  2. Select payment category as “Courses/Seminar/Workshop”
  3. Select name of the event as “National Conference on Wind Engineering”
  4. Fill in the further required details and click on the SUBMIT button.
  5. Make the payment.
  6. After making the payment, kindly save the registration receipt for future reference.
  7. Fill the required payment details in the NCWE-25 Registration Form to confirm your registration.
